Additional Stock Data Visualization

Research and Results

In this project, we performed an analysis of the stock data for Schneider Electric, focusing on years 2012 and 2013. Our objective was to analyze key metrics such as closing prices, volumes, and the high-low price range to understand the stock's performance.

Key Findings

  • Closing Prices: The stock showed consistent growth from 2012 to 2013. In 2012, the highest closing price reached 69.60 INR, while in 2013, it went up to 79.60 INR, indicating a positive trend in the company's stock value.
  • Trading Volume: The trading volume remained relatively stable, with some spikes during certain months. Higher trading volumes indicate increased investor interest in the stock.
  • High-Low Price Range: The stock's high prices reached as much as 81.25 INR in 2013, while the lowest price remained above 70 INR, showing strong support levels and investor confidence.

Methodology

We used Chart.js to visualize the data and provide interactive elements. The dashboard allows users to:

  • Select different years to view data for specific periods.
  • Switch between chart types (line, bar, and pie) to gain different perspectives on the data.
  • View additional visualizations such as trading volumes.

Conclusion

The analysis suggests that Schneider Electric's stock experienced significant growth between 2012 and 2013. This analysis can serve as a valuable resource for investors seeking to understand the stock's performance over time. Further analysis could include comparing Schneider Electric's performance with its competitors or analyzing external market factors that influenced these trends.
